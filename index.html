Project Overview

Bridging the gap between ecological preservation and infrastructure thermal comfort requirements.

The Problem

  • Traditional AC architectures demand unsustainable electrical load metrics.
  • High operating margins impact low-to-middle income accessibility tiers.
  • Global warming accelerated via HVAC hydrofluorocarbon emissions leaks.

Our Solution

  • Hybrid two-tier layout balances high latent cooling efficiency vectors.
  • Minimal components maximize structural runtime structural life expectancy profiles.
  • Eliminates harmful fluid chemical refrigerants entirely from circulation.

Interactive Working Principle

Trace the sequential transformations as air processes through the sustainable system array.

1

Ambient Intake induction

Thermal loads collect structural perimeter air masses into the specialized entry funnel module.

2

Honeycomb Saturation Array

Air interacts directly with water films cross-layering the cross-fluted cellulose paper matrices.

3

Primary Stage DC Fan Pull

Low tension distribution motors stabilize flow rate velocities through transition spaces.

4

Silica Gel Adsorption Chamber

Synthetic porous silicon dioxides trap saturated vapor molecules via chemical affinity interfaces.

5

Final Output Stage Discharge

Secondary propulsion components deliver refreshed, cool, optimized relative humidity currents outwards.
