System Architecture

The Core Challenge

Bridging the gap between thermal comfort and environmental responsibility.

The Problem

High Electricity Consumption: Conventional ACs account for massive grid strain globally.

Environmental Impact: Refrigerants (HFCs) severely contribute to global warming.

High Operating Cost: Prohibitive utility bills for sustained cooling during summers.

Our Solution

VAYU reimagines thermodynamics for the built environment. By coupling evaporative cooling with advanced adsorption techniques, we strip out energy-heavy compressors entirely.


The result is a hybrid passive system that delivers crisp, comfortable air while virtually eliminating greenhouse gas emissions and drastically cutting operational expenditure.
